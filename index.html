Welcome to the Civilian Awareness System!


With government and tech companies increasingly expanding their reach to every corner of the globe this application will seek to counter this. Users of this system will only be able to view your local area.
You will be able to see all events anyone has added locally, whether it be traffic, social or a food event going on.
You can add your own events to the map my clicking the menu in the top left of the screen and clicking add event.
A form will then be visible on your screen asking you to add details of the event and select the co-ordinates by clicking the button and pressing on the map where you want to plot your event and then submit and thats all there is!
You can filter the events you see by clicking the show last 24Hours button also in the top left of the screen. After you have had a play around with the map and application if you can fill out this form and tell me what you think and any feedback you have and if you like the concept.
